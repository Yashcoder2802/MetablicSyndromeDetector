Hello! Lets Get Started

Welcome to MetaPred!, a risk assessment system for specifically metabolic syndrome. Our website is designed to help you assess your risk of developing metabolic syndrome, a group of conditions that increases the risk of cardiovascular disease and type 2 diabetes. By providing an easy-to-use assessment tool, educational resources, and guidance on next steps, our website MetaPred can help individuals assess their risk of developing metabolic syndrome and take steps to prevent this condition.
